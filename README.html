Privacy Policy

Welcome to Concentrix. This privacy policy explains how our app, Concentrix, handles your data and ensures your privacy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Concentrix is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from our users. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function. There are no data collection mechanisms in place within the app.

2. No Internet Required

Concentrix does not need an internet connection for its core functionalities. All features and interactions within the app are processed locally on your device. This ensures that no data is transmitted or shared over the internet.

3. Push Notifications

Concentrix utilizes push notifications to enhance user experience by providing timely reminders and updates. These notifications are generated within the app and do not involve any personal data. You can control and manage these notifications through your device settings.

4. Data Security

Although we do not collect any user data, we are committed to ensuring that Concentrix is secure. The app is designed following best security practices to prevent any unauthorized access or vulnerabilities. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
